Embroidery is a great way to add a personal touch to your clothing. Whether you’re looking to add a logo to a shirt for a business, or just want to add a special message to a gift, embroidery is a great way to make your clothing unique. But how much does it cost to get a shirt embroidered?

The cost of embroidery depends on a few factors, such as the size of the design, the number of colors used, and the type of fabric. Generally, the cost of embroidery is based on the number of stitches used in the design. The more stitches, the more expensive the embroidery will be. Additionally, the cost of the shirt itself will also factor into the total cost.

The cost of embroidery can range from a few dollars for a small design to hundreds of dollars for a large, complex design. How much does it cost to get a shirt embroidered? It really depends on the size and complexity of the design, as well as the cost of the shirt itself. If you’re looking for a ballpark estimate, you can expect to pay anywhere from $10 to $50 for a basic design.
