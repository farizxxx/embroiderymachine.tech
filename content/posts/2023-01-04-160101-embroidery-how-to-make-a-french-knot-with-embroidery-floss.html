How to Make a French Knot with Embroidery Floss

A French knot is a classic embroidery stitch that adds texture and dimension to any project. It is a great way to add a bit of extra detail to your work. With a few simple steps, you can easily learn how to make a French knot with embroidery floss.

To begin, you will need a needle and embroidery floss. Start by threading the needle with the floss. Then, bring the needle up from the back of the fabric and wrap the floss around the needle two or three times. Next, insert the needle back into the fabric, close to where it came up. Pull the needle and floss through the fabric, but be sure to keep the wraps around the needle. Finally, pull the needle and floss tight to create the knot.

Once you have mastered the French knot, you can use it to add texture and dimension to any embroidery project. You can also use it to create a variety of patterns and designs. With a bit of practice, you will be able to create beautiful and intricate designs with the French knot.
