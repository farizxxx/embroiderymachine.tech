How to Embroider a Circle by Hand

Embroidering a circle by hand is a great way to add a unique touch to any project. Whether you’re making a quilt, a pillow, or a wall hanging, a hand-embroidered circle can be the perfect finishing touch. With a few simple supplies and a bit of practice, you can easily learn how to embroider a circle by hand.

To begin, you’ll need a hoop, embroidery floss, a needle, and a piece of fabric. Place the fabric in the hoop and tighten it so that the fabric is taut. Then, thread the needle with the embroidery floss and knot the end. To create the circle, start by making a small knot in the center of the fabric. Then, make a series of small, evenly spaced stitches around the knot, gradually increasing the size of the circle. When you reach the desired size, make a few more stitches to secure the circle and knot the thread at the back of the fabric.

Once you’ve mastered the basics of embroidering a circle, you can experiment with different stitches and colors to create unique designs. You can also use the same technique to embroider other shapes, such as stars, hearts, and flowers. With a bit of practice, you’ll be able to create beautiful embroidered circles for all your projects.
