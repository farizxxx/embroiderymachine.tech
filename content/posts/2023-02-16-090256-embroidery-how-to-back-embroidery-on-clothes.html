With a few simple steps, you can easily add a beautiful and unique design to any piece of clothing.

The first step in back embroidery is to choose the fabric you want to use. You’ll want to choose a fabric that is thick enough to support the embroidery, but not so thick that it will be difficult to work with. Once you’ve chosen the fabric, you’ll need to cut it to the size and shape you want. Make sure to leave enough fabric around the edges to be able to fold it over and secure it in place.

Next, you’ll need to transfer your design onto the fabric. You can do this by tracing the design onto the fabric with a pencil or using a transfer paper. Once the design is transferred, you’ll need to secure the fabric in place. You can do this by using a hoop or frame, or by basting the fabric in place.
