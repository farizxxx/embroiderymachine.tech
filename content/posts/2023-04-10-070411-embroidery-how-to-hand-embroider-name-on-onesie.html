Hand embroidering a name on a onesie is a great way to personalize a gift for a special someone. It is a simple and creative way to show someone you care. With a few basic supplies and a bit of patience, you can create a unique and beautiful piece of art.

To get started, you will need a onesie, embroidery floss, an embroidery needle, and a fabric marker. Begin by using the fabric marker to draw the name onto the onesie. Make sure the letters are evenly spaced and the lines are straight. Once the name is drawn, thread the needle with the embroidery floss. Start with a knot at the end of the floss and then begin to stitch the letters. Use a simple backstitch to create the letters. Make sure to keep the stitches even and the same size. When you are finished, tie off the floss with a knot and trim the excess.

How to Hand Embroider Name on Onesie:

  • Gather supplies: onesie, embroidery floss, embroidery needle, and fabric marker
  • Draw the name onto the onesie with the fabric marker
  • Thread the needle with the embroidery floss and tie a knot at the end
  • Use a backstitch to create the letters
  • Keep the stitches even and the same size
  • Tie off the floss with a knot and trim the excess
