How to Embroider by Hand on Clothes

Embroidery is a great way to add a personal touch to your clothing. Whether you’re looking to add a unique design to a t-shirt, or you want to add a special message to a gift, embroidery is a great way to make your clothing stand out. With a few simple supplies and a bit of practice, you can easily learn how to embroider by hand on clothes.

To get started, you’ll need a few supplies. You’ll need an embroidery hoop, embroidery floss, a needle, and a fabric marker. The embroidery hoop will help keep your fabric taut while you work, and the fabric marker will help you draw your design onto the fabric. Once you have your supplies, you’ll need to decide on a design. You can find embroidery patterns online, or you can create your own. Once you’ve chosen your design, use the fabric marker to draw it onto the fabric.
