Can you use felt for embroidery? The answer is yes! Felt is a great material for embroidery projects. It’s easy to work with and comes in a variety of colors and textures. Plus, it’s relatively inexpensive, so you can get a lot of bang for your buck. Felt is also durable, so your embroidery project will last for years to come. When it comes to embroidery, felt is a great choice.
