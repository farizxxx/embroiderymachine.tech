How to Start Crewel Embroidery

Crewel embroidery is a type of surface embroidery that uses wool yarn to create beautiful designs. It is a great way to add a unique touch to clothing, home decor, and other projects. To get started with crewel embroidery, you will need a few basic supplies.

First, you will need a piece of fabric to work on. A lightweight cotton or linen fabric is best for crewel embroidery. You will also need a hoop to hold the fabric
