Embroidery floss friendship bracelets with beads are a fun and creative way to show your friends how much you care. Whether you’re making them for yourself or for a special someone, these bracelets are sure to bring a smile to anyone’s face. With a few simple supplies, you can create a unique and beautiful bracelet that will last for years to come.

To make an embroidery floss friendship bracelet with beads, you’ll need embroidery floss, beads, scissors, and a needle. Start by cutting the embroidery floss into four equal lengths. Then, thread the needle with one of the strands and begin stringing the beads onto the floss. Once you’ve strung the desired number of beads, tie the ends of the floss together to form a loop. Finally, tie the remaining three strands of floss to the loop and begin braiding the strands together. When you’ve reached the desired length, tie the ends together to complete the bracelet.
