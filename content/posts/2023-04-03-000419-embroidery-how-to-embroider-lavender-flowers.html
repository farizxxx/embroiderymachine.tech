How to Embroider Lavender Flowers

First, choose the right fabric. A lightweight, tightly woven fabric is best for embroidering lavender flowers. This will help ensure that the stitches stay in place and the design looks neat and even. You can also use a stabilizer to help keep the fabric from stretching or puckering.
