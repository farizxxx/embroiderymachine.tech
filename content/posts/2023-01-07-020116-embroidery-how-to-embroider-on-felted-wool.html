Embroidering on felted wool is a great way to add a unique touch to your projects! Whether you’re making a cozy blanket, a stylish hat, or a beautiful wall hanging, felted wool is the perfect material to add a bit of texture and color. But how do you go about embroidering on felted wool? It’s not as hard as it looks!

First, you’ll need to choose the right type of felted wool. Look for a wool that is thick and sturdy, as this will make it easier to work with. You’ll also want to make sure that the wool is not too thick, as this can make it difficult to stitch through. Once you’ve chosen the right wool, you’ll need to prepare it for embroidery. Start by laying the wool flat on a hard surface and using a ruler to make sure that it is straight. Then, use a sharp needle to poke holes in the wool where you want to stitch. This will make it easier to stitch through the wool.

Now that your wool is ready, it’s time to start embroidering! Choose a thread that is the same color as the wool, or a contrasting color for a more dramatic effect. Start by threading the needle and knotting the end of the thread. Then, use a simple running stitch to embroider your design. You can also use other stitches, such as the back stitch or the French knot, to add texture and detail to your design. When you’re finished, knot the end of the thread and trim the excess.

Embroidering on felted wool is a great way to add a unique touch to your projects. With a bit of practice, you’ll be able to create beautiful designs that will last for years to come!
